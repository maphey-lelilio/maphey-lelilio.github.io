Cornflake Cakes

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 3 Tbsp
Sugar 200g
Honey 1 Tbsp
Cornflakes 4 Cups

Step 1: Preheat oven to 150C/300F.

Step 2: Heat the butter, sugar and honey in a small saucepan until frothy, then remove from the heat.

Step 3: Add Cornflakes and mix well.

Step 4: Spoon into cupcake cases and bake for 10 minutes. Remove from the oven and leave to cool before handing them out.

Chocolaate-Chip-Cookies

  • Time:15 minutes
  • Serves:10
Ingredients Quantity
Coaster Sugar 225g
Melted Butter 200g
Plain Flour 300g
Baking Powder 1 Tsp
Salt One Pinch
Vanilla Extract 1 Tsp
Chocolate Chips 100g-200g
Egg 1

Step 1: Heat the oven to 160C (140C fan, Gas 3). Line a baking tray with parchment paper or lightly grease with butter.

Step 2: Put the sugar and melted butter in a bowl and mix together with a wooden spoon.

Step 3: Sift the flour, baking powder and salt together and mix into the sugar and butter mixture.

Step 4: Add the vanilla and chocolate chips and use your hands to distribute evenly and mix together. Once you get a dough texture, add the egg and knead using your hands again until combined.

Step 5: Portion the dough into 20 balls. Put on the baking tray and flatten a little. Keep the balls small and leave plenty of space between each one as they will spread. You might prefer to bake in batches or use two baking trays.

Step 6: Bake for 10-20 minutes until lightly golden and golden at the edges. Leave to cool on the baking tray for 10 minutes, or if you have time until completely cool.
